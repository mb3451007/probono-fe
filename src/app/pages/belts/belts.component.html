Explanation of belts and stars

After every volunteerâ€™s statistics on the Volunteers page, you can see either a belt or a star. These reflect the contributions of volunteers to the program.

Belts: Belts are awarded in direct relation to the number of words translated (with one hour of interpreting equivalent to 1,000 words). The more words you have done, the higher your belt, as in a karate belt system.

Here are the values for acquiring each belt and the reward:

Stars: Stars are awarded for non-translation or interpreting contributions, such as being an Ambassador or a Ninja, or any other contribution at the discretion of the program team.

Color Scheme: Just like belts, stars are colored according to quantity.
